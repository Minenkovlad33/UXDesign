ABOUT THE EVENT

  • UX Design MeetUP is dedicated to bringing together professionals, enthusiasts, or simply anyone who is interested in how to deliver outstanding user experiences and find the best tools and practices for evaluating and collecting user requirements.

  • It is a great opportunity to deepen your knowledge in terms of approaches, methodology, and overall work style, as well as gain hands-on experience during the workshop.

  • You will love it if you are a UX practitioner, product manager, front-end developer, marketing specialist, or just getting started with web design.
